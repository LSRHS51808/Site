• There are three main image formats on the web; PNG, GIF and JPEG.
  • A PNG is a photo with a transparent background, great for when you want to have the image blend in with the background of your site.
  • A GIF is an often animated image that plays on a 2-3 second cycle. A Gif is great for when you want to make your site feel alive.
  • A JPEG is just a regular photo either taken with a digital camera or made on photoshop.
