How We Make and Refine our Yogurt

This is where you will find our complex process of making the yogurts. Our tedious process is unlike any other yogurt maker's.

Our refining process is unlike any other. First, we pick the fruit right from the tree, then we send it off to get the approval of quality control. After this is done, we start making the yogurt based on the fruit. We can sense the fruit's attitude and that will determine how many live cultures will go into the yogurt. Let's say we have a very rude and angry with the world strawberry, this will make the yogurt sour so therefore we need to add more live cultures to balance it out. This is why we have the tastiest yogurt in the world.
